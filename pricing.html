Which one should I choose?

If you want to fix one area of your business quickly — pricing, branding, clients or marketing — start with a 5-week track at ₦35,000. If you want to rebuild the whole business with a 3-month commitment, capstone and graduation, go for the Mini-MBA.

Can I upgrade from a track to the Mini-MBA?

Yes. If you complete a track and want to continue with the full Mini-MBA, we will credit your track fee toward the Mini-MBA price. Talk to us before you enrol to set this up.
