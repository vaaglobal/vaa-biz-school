  • The exact reason talented creatives stay broke — and the one positioning shift that changes everything
  • How to choose a profitable niche (bridal, product, corporate, content creation) so you stop competing on price and start winning on fit
  • How to define your visual style and brand voice so the right clients recognise you instantly and the wrong ones self-select out
  • A simple logo, colour palette and portfolio layout that signals premium — without hiring a designer
  • How to write a bio and service description that sells your value before a client has even seen your work
  • Day rates, package rates and project rates — which model earns you more, and when to switch

  • Local SEO — how to rank for searches like "photographer in Lagos" or "graphic designer in Abuja" for free
  • A done-for-you contract template that stops non-payers, limits scope creep, and protects every job from day one
  • Deposit policies and follow-up systems that filter out time-wasters before they waste your time
  • How to save from every job and plan your income so January is never a panic month again

Questions about this track

Is this just for photographers?+
Not at all. The track is built for every kind of creative service provider — photographers, videographers, graphic designers, event decorators, screen printers and content creators. The business problems are the same across all of them: underpricing, inconsistent clients, no financial system. We fix all of it, whatever your craft.
I have no clients yet. Can I still join?+
Yes — and this might actually be the best time to join. Week 2 is dedicated entirely to landing your first paying clients, even with a thin portfolio. You will leave with a clear offer, a way to present it, and a system for getting your first real bookings.
How technical is this? I am not a business person.+
There is nothing technical here. No accounting software, no spreadsheets you need a degree to understand. If you can send a WhatsApp voice note and manage your own DMs, you have every skill this track requires. Everything is practical and immediately usable.
