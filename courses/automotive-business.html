Your trade is not the problem. Your business structure is.

The mechanic who charges ₦80,000 for a job and the one who charges ₦30,000 for the same job are equally skilled. The difference is not competence — it is brand, pricing, and how they are found. This track closes that gap for auto and tech repair businesses in Nigeria:

  • Auto mechanics and technicians — great work, unpredictable income, customers who negotiate after the job is done
  • Auto electricians — diagnosing faults others can't find, with no system to charge diagnostic fees or earn repeat business
  • Panel beaters and spray painters — booked one week, empty the next, because the pipeline is 100% referral and 0% system
  • Phone and laptop repairers — high volume, thin margins, and customers who disappear after one repair and take their friends elsewhere
  • Vehicle detailers and car wash operators — loyal to customers who are not loyal back, and no process to change that

If your business fits any of these — this track is the intervention.

What does your business look like after this track?

Here is the specific transformation this track delivers — not concepts, not frameworks, not ideas. Actual systems, live in your business before Week 5 ends:

MODULE 01WEEKS 1 & 2 — Brand, Pricing & Business Identity+

Before: Customers see a workshop. After: They see a brand — and brands command higher prices, get more referrals, and are trusted before a single word is spoken.

  • Complete workshop brand identity — business name, logo brief, colour system, and brand guidelines built during Week 1, ready to apply to your signage, receipts, and WhatsApp business profile immediately
  • Diagnostic fee policy — a written, professional system that lets you charge ₦2,000–₦5,000 to assess any vehicle or device before repair work begins. The same expertise that currently earns you nothing during "free inspection" becomes a revenue line from your next job
  • Parts-plus-labour pricing model — a formula that calculates your true cost on every job and sets a floor on your margin, so you never again take home less than you should because parts prices changed or a job ran long
  • Customer value communication script — what to say to a customer who doesn't understand engines, circuits, or why your fee is what it is — so they stop questioning the bill and start seeing you as the expert you already are
  • Dispute handling system — a calm, professional response to any price negotiation or complaint that protects your margin and your reputation at the same time, without a single argument
MODULE 02WEEKS 3, 4 & 5 — Customer Pipeline, Marketing & Business Finance+

Before: New customers find you by accident. After: They find you on Google, they come back on a schedule, and they bring other people — because you have a system running in the background that makes all of this automatic.

  • Live Google Maps presence — your Google Business Profile built and optimised together in Week 3, putting your business in front of every vehicle owner and phone user searching nearby. This is free, it generates calls 24 hours a day, and it compounds over time
  • 5-star review system — a repeatable process for collecting Google reviews from satisfied customers at exactly the right moment — the reviews that rank your business above competitors in local search without spending on ads
  • Inbound call strategy (zero ad spend) — the specific local SEO steps that drive enquiries from Google organically. Unlike paid ads, these keep working after you stop paying attention to them
  • Targeted Facebook and Instagram ads — how to run ads reaching car owners and phone users in your city for under ₦5,000 a week: what to say, who to target, and exactly how to measure whether your money is working
  • WhatsApp customer retention system — a follow-up sequence built in Week 4 that automatically re-engages past customers at 30, 60, and 90 days. Customers who intended to return but forgot become customers who actually do. Referrals start coming in without you asking for them
  • Fleet and corporate client pipeline — how to identify, approach, and close logistics companies, corporate fleets, and ride-hailing drivers for steady, predictable, high-volume work. One fleet account eliminates the unpredictability of walk-in income entirely
  • Business finance tracking system — a simple daily sheet that shows your real profit per job, per week, per month — not just the cash that passed through your hands. You cannot grow a business whose numbers you do not know

Questions about this track

Does this cover both auto mechanics and phone repairers?+
Yes — because the business problem is the same regardless of what you repair. Whether the job is an engine or a screen, the issues are identical: no brand, no pricing system, no way to bring customers back. Every module is taught with both businesses in mind. Every example covers both. There is no one who is a secondary audience in this track — both are primary.
My operation is small — just me, working from a roadside spot. Does this still apply?+
It applies most to you. A one-person operation at a roadside location is 100% dependent on whoever walks past or was told about you. After this track, you will have a Google listing sending people to you while you work, a pricing system protecting every job, and a follow-up process converting satisfied customers into repeat clients and referrals. You do not need a big workshop to run a business that operates like one. You need the right systems — which is exactly what this track builds.

We also cover direct fleet outreach and referral activation — both of which work entirely offline. You pick the channels that suit your business.
